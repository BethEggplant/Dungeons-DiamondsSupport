Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock the full game?
The first 5 seasons are free. After that, a one-time $1.99 purchase unlocks the full game permanently — no subscriptions, no ads.

I purchased the game but it's not unlocked.
Tap "Restore Purchases" on the paywall screen to recover your purchase.

My save data is missing.
Save data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app will remove your save. We recommend not deleting the app unless necessary.

The app crashed or I found a bug.
Please email us at beth.eggplant96@gmail.com with a description of what happened and what device/iOS version you're using.

Can I play on iPad?
Dungeons & Diamonds is designed for iPhone only.

Privacy Policy
Dungeons & Diamonds does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All game data is saved locally on your device.
